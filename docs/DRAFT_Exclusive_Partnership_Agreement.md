## RECITALS

A. DeWater Products is engaged in the business of supplying industrial pipe fittings, couplings, valves, expansion joints, strainers, and related products to the Australian market.

B. Red Key Digital provides web development, digital marketing, search engine optimisation (SEO), and related digital services.

C. The Principal is a director and/or controlling shareholder of Red Key Digital.

D. The Parties wish to enter into an exclusive partnership arrangement whereby Red Key Digital and the Principal agree not to provide services to competitors of DeWater Products.

---

## AGREED TERMS

### 1. DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION

**1.1** In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires:

"**Competitor**" means any business, entity, or person (whether direct or indirect) that:
   - (a) manufactures, distributes, supplies, or sells industrial pipe fittings, couplings, valves, expansion joints, strainers, pipe repair clamps, flange adaptors, or similar fluid control products in Australia; or
   - (b) operates in the same or substantially similar market segment as DeWater Products; or
   - (c) is reasonably likely to compete with DeWater Products for customers in the industrial piping and fluid control sector.

"**Confidential Information**" means all information relating to the business, operations, customers, suppliers, pricing, strategies, and trade secrets of DeWater Products.

"**Services**" means web development, website maintenance, hosting, digital marketing, SEO, content creation, graphic design, and any related digital or marketing services.

"**Territory**" means Australia and any other jurisdiction where DeWater Products conducts business.

### 2. EXCLUSIVE PARTNERSHIP

**2.1** Red Key Digital and the Principal agree that, for the duration of this Agreement:

   (a) They will not provide any Services to any Competitor of DeWater Products;

   (b) They will not enter into any agreement, arrangement, or understanding with any Competitor;

   (c) They will not solicit, accept, or undertake any work from any Competitor;

   (d) They will promptly notify DeWater Products if approached by any Competitor seeking Services.

**2.2** This exclusivity obligation applies to:
   - (a) Red Key Digital as a corporate entity;
   - (b) The Principal personally;
   - (c) Any related entity, subsidiary, or associated business of Red Key Digital or the Principal;
   - (d) Any subcontractors engaged by Red Key Digital.

### 3. SCOPE OF COMPETITORS

**3.1** Without limiting the generality of Clause 1.1, the following categories of businesses are considered Competitors:

   (a) **Direct Competitors:** Businesses that sell substantially similar products including but not limited to:
   - Pipe couplings and connectors
   - Industrial valves (butterfly, check, gate, ball, foot, float valves)
   - Expansion joints and rubber bellows
   - Pipe repair clamps
   - Strainers and filters
   - Flange adaptors

   (b) **Brand Competitors:** Australian distributors or retailers of competing brands to those distributed by DeWater Products, including but not limited to distributors of non-DeWater-authorised Straub, Orbit, Teekay, or similar product lines.

   (c) **Indirect Competitors:** Businesses whose primary or substantial revenue derives from industrial pipe fittings and fluid control products, even if their product range differs from DeWater Products.

**3.2** DeWater Products may, from time to time, provide Red Key Digital with a list of specific businesses considered Competitors, which shall be incorporated into this Agreement by reference.

**3.3** If Red Key Digital is uncertain whether a prospective client constitutes a Competitor, they shall seek written clarification from DeWater Products prior to engaging with such prospective client.

### 4. TERM AND TERMINATION

**4.1** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for the duration of the business partnership between Red Key Digital and DeWater Products ("Partnership Period").

**4.2** The Partnership Period shall be deemed to continue for so long as Red Key Digital provides any Services to DeWater Products or maintains any active systems, websites, or digital assets on behalf of DeWater Products.

**4.3** Upon termination or expiry of the Partnership Period, the exclusivity obligations in Clause 2 shall continue to apply for a further period of **twelve (12) months** ("Post-Termination Period").

**4.4** Either Party may terminate the Partnership Period by giving not less than ninety (90) days written notice to the other Party.

**4.5** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if:
   - (a) The other Party commits a material breach of this Agreement and fails to remedy such breach within thirty (30) days of receiving written notice; or
   - (b) The other Party becomes insolvent, enters into liquidation, or has a receiver appointed.

**4.6** For the avoidance of doubt, termination of the Partnership Period does not terminate the Post-Termination Period obligations, which shall continue to apply for the full twelve (12) month period following the end of the Partnership Period.

### 5. CONSIDERATION

**5.1** The Parties acknowledge and agree that:

   (a) No exclusivity fee or additional monetary consideration is payable under this Agreement;

   (b) The ongoing business relationship between the Parties, including DeWater Products' engagement of Red Key Digital to provide Services, constitutes good and valuable consideration for the exclusivity obligations undertaken by Red Key Digital and the Principal;

   (c) Red Key Digital and the Principal enter into this Agreement voluntarily and acknowledge the commercial benefits of the exclusive partnership arrangement.

**5.2** For the avoidance of doubt, Red Key Digital shall continue to invoice DeWater Products for Services rendered in accordance with separately agreed rates and terms, which are independent of this Agreement.

### 6. CONSEQUENCES OF BREACH

**6.1** Red Key Digital and the Principal acknowledge that a breach of the exclusivity provisions in Clause 2 would cause significant and irreparable harm to DeWater Products.

**6.2** In the event of a breach or threatened breach, DeWater Products shall be entitled to:
   - (a) Seek injunctive or other equitable relief without the need to prove actual damage;
   - (b) Terminate this Agreement immediately;
   - (c) Claim damages for any loss suffered as a result of the breach;
   - (d) Recover any fees paid to Red Key Digital during the period of the breach.

**6.3** The obligations in this Clause 6 survive termination of this Agreement.

### 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

**7.1** Red Key Digital and the Principal shall keep confidential all Confidential Information of DeWater Products and shall not disclose such information to any third party without prior written consent.

**7.2** This confidentiality obligation survives termination of this Agreement indefinitely.

### 8. WARRANTIES AND REPRESENTATIONS

**8.1** Red Key Digital and the Principal warrant and represent that:
   - (a) As at the Effective Date, they are not providing Services to any Competitor;
   - (b) They have full authority to enter into this Agreement;
   - (c) This Agreement does not conflict with any other agreement to which they are a party.

### 9. GENERAL PROVISIONS

**9.1 Entire Agreement:** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements relating to its subject matter.

**9.2 Amendment:** This Agreement may only be amended in writing signed by all Parties.

**9.3 Waiver:** A waiver of any provision of this Agreement is only effective if it is in writing and signed by the Party granting the waiver.

**9.4 Severability:** If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

**9.5 Governing Law:** This Agreement is governed by the laws of Western Australia, and the Parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Western Australia.

**9.6 Notices:** Any notice under this Agreement must be in writing and delivered to the addresses set out above or as otherwise notified in writing.

**9.7 Assignment:** Red Key Digital and the Principal may not assign or transfer any rights or obligations under this Agreement without the prior written consent of DeWater Products.
